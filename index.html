About Us

We are a family owned and operated full service landscaping company. We are located in Harrison, Michigan. We serve all locations in Clare, Isabella, and Gladwin counties. No job is too big or too small.

We have a dual pricing system. We calculate fees by the hour and by job quote. You pay whichever works out to be the most economical for you. Saving you "$$".
