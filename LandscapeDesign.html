Horticulturalist:

The science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers or ornamental plants.

At Doug Landscaping, we have two horticulturalists on staff. Let our experts recommend plants most suited for your location. We can test your soil and apply needed fertilizer and show you plants that will thrive in your garden.

We offer a large selection of annual flowers, perennials, ornamental plants, and grasses. If you are a vegetable or fruit grower, our experts can recommend varietals especially for you.

Have a tree that looks sickly? Here at Doug Lanscaping we have an arborist on staff. Let our specialist care for your trees, recommend trimming, feeding, or recommend new species especially for you.
